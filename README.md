SuperStore Sales Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

The SuperStore Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, shipping modes, and regional distribution of sales. The dashboard provides valuable insights into business operations, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions by monitoring key performance indicators (KPIs) and identifying sales patterns across different regions.


---

🎯 Objectives

Track overall sales, orders, and profit.

Analyze monthly sales and profit trends.

Compare sales performance across regions and states.

Evaluate customer segments and payment modes.

Monitor shipping methods and delivery efficiency.

Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories.

📈 Key Insights

The Consumer Segment contributes the highest share of sales.

Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most preferred payment method.

The West Region generates the highest sales among all regions.

Office Supplies is the top-performing category.

Standard Class Shipping is the most frequently used shipping mode.

Sales and profit show significant growth toward the end of the year.
